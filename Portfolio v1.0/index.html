About Me

Hi, welcome to my portfolio. I'm Rezaur Rahman, a full-stack dev from Dhaka, Bangladesh. Currently I'm studying Computer Science & Engineering at Daffodil International University. I have great passion for web technologies and android apps. To me software development is a modern from of art. I aspire to become one of the most recognized Java Web App Developer in BD.

I also like to teach programming to my juniors. During my academic semesters, I volunteer as teaching assistant (prefect) or take semester long training classes online to teach newbie juniors C/Java programming. Sometimes I volunteer as a trainer of C Khoon Junior program at our university's programming club DIU CPC. Where we teach C programming to the absolute beginners.

I also make programming related tutorials on my YouTube channel Dev Rezaur.
